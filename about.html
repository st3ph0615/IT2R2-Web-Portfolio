About Me

Hi, I'm Stephanie! I’m a web designer with a passion for creating beautiful, user-friendly designs using Figma. I specialize in crafting clean, intuitive websites and apps that not only look stunning but also offer a seamless user experience.

In addition to design, I have a working knowledge of HTML and CSS, allowing me to bring my designs to life with well-structured, responsive layouts. This helps ensure that the transition from design to development is smooth and efficient.

I believe that great design should be simple, functional, and enjoyable for everyone. If you're looking for a designer who understands both aesthetics and usability, I’d love to collaborate with you! Feel free to reach out—let’s bring your ideas to life.

Skills: HTML, CSS, UI/UX Design
